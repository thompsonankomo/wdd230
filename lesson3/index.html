Purpose Statement

The main objective of the Bulawayo Chamber Of Commerce is to provide services and opportunities for growth to all small businesses and commercial businesses in and around the communities of Bulawayo, Gweru and Masvingo. We further the interests of locally owned businesses first to improve the economic welfare of the community and for the betterment of our indigenous people. As the community expands and grows there are great opportunities and challenges that needs the expertise of collective problem solving.It is anticipated that a further 1,5 million people will relocate to Bulawayo including Businesses that would wanyt to explore the rcih diversity that we enjoy in this City. Should you feel inclined to join we invite you to join this Chamber of Commerce and be part of the Dynamic that will ch change the outlook of the City in terms of Business and Technology Advancements.
