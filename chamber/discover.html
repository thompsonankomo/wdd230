Chipangali Wildlife Orphanage

The Chipangali wildlife orphanage is a beautiful place which caters for wild animals, snakes and crocodiles.
Located in the vicinity of the city of Bulawayo, the Place is a pride of the City as it atttracts a lot of
foreign tourists. You will find all the wild animals that are found in the African Savannahs including the desert. A
great place to visit with family and have great time.

Khami Ruins In Bulawayo

This an Ancient edifice and is well known for attracting Foreign tourists. It is believed that the first
King of the Ndebeles used to stay there together with his wives. Becausse of this rich History the place has become popular among ,
the locals and is a great place for family activities and more importantly engage the children on the roots of the land of Zimbabwe especially
Bulawayo and the surrounding communities

Nesbitt Castle Bulawayo

This place is a darling among the locals as it is a place where cultural events take place. It is a great meeting place for the
founding fathers of the city. Local Artists use the place to host their shows and engage with their customres
Furthermore Investors and Local Business people gather there for their caucus meeting. It is great place to go and attend
s ome of these self reliance initiatives that can benefit generations to come

Joshua Mqabuko Nkomo Airport

The Bulawayo Airport is a majestic edifice that was built to connect international flights to the city. This project happened two years ago
as the Government solidified tourism efforts in the City. It is located just ten kilometers from the Coity center and is a main hub of activity for the
City. It was named after the founding Father of Zimbabwe, Dr Joshua Nkomo. He resided in the city and palyed an inspiring role is abolishing the evils
of the settler regime. being known as Mqabuko airport this has seen the City having been the hive of activity in the past years.
