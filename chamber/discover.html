The Victoria Falls

The beautiful Victoria Falls named after Queen Victoria of England are one of the seven natural wonders of the world. This place brings tourists like no other place in Zimbabwe and we are pleased that our community hosts the beautiful and majestic wonder.

Chipangali Wildlife Orphanage , Bulawayo, Zimbabwe

The Chipangai Wildlife orphanage is located just 10km outside the outskirts off Bulawayo. It is a place of resort as it accommodates both local and foreign tourist. Established in the early 1900s to cater for wildlife that found itself stranded , this place has become a hub of wildlfie viewing. With animals ranging from leopards, Lions, to reptiles like the crocodile. Come and enjoy the beautiful scenery and enjoy the wonderful lessons about African wildlife from our wardens
