How To Join Us

  1. Show proof of being a Credible Business person or group operating within jurisdiction
  2. Obtain the chambers application form from the Head office along Selbourne way.
  3. Complete the application form from our form portal and then post it or drop it in person at our offices.
  4. Attend a briefing meeting on invitation.
